Terms of Service

Last Updated: May 2025

1. Account Registration

To access JH FileTransfer services, you must register for an account. You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration and to update such information to keep it accurate, current, and complete.

2. Service Description

JH FileTransfer provides a secure file sharing and project management platform for professionals. Our service allows:

  • Creation of projects with client members
  • Department-level collaboration for companies
  • Integrated communication tools (direct and group messaging)
  • Secure client access via project ID and passkey
  • Payment escrow services with 1% transaction fee
  • Financial tracking and reporting

3. Acceptable Use

You agree not to use the Service to:

  • Upload, store, or share illegal, harmful, or infringing content
  • Violate any laws or third-party rights
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to systems or data
  • Disrupt the integrity or performance of the Service
  • Use the Service for any high-risk activities where failure could lead to damage

4. Payment Terms

All payments are processed through our secure payment gateway. We charge a 1% service fee on all transactions. Refunds are available within 24 hours of account activation for unsatisfied customers.

5. Termination

Accounts may be terminated for violation of these terms. Upon termination, all uploaded files will be permanently deleted from our servers.

1. Data Protection

JH FileTransfer implements industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for all file transfers and storage
  • Two-factor authentication for sensitive actions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with physical access controls

2. Authentication

We require two-step verification for:

  • Account registration
  • Password resets
  • Account changes made through customer service
  • Sensitive financial operations

3. Access Control

Each project member receives unique credentials:

  • Individual passkeys for all users
  • Project-specific access controls
  • Detailed activity logs showing all access
  • Automatic session timeouts after inactivity

4. Data Retention

Your data is handled as follows:

  • All uploaded files are encrypted at rest
  • Files are permanently deleted upon account termination
  • Activity logs are retained for 90 days
  • Financial records are kept for your membersip period for compliance
